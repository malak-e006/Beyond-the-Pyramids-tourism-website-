Timeless Egypt: Cairo, Luxor & Nile Cruise

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo

Arrive at Cairo International Airport. Your personal guide will greet you and assist with visa and luggage. Transfer to your 5-star hotel overlooking the Nile. Evening welcome dinner with traditional Egyptian cuisine and folklore show.

Dinner included

Day 2: The Great Pyramids & Egyptian Museum

Full-day tour of the Giza Plateau to see the Great Pyramid of Khufu, Pyramid of Khafre, and the mysterious Sphinx. Visit the Solar Boat Museum. Afternoon at the Egyptian Museum of Antiquities, home of King Tutankhamun's treasures.

Breakfast & Lunch included

Day 3: Fly to Luxor & East Bank Temples

Morning flight to Luxor, the world's greatest open-air museum. Explore Karnak Temple, the largest religious complex ever built, and Luxor Temple illuminated at night. Board your 5-star Nile cruise ship for the next 4 nights.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner included

Day 4: Valley of the Kings & Hatshepsut Temple

Cross to the West Bank to explore the Valley of the Kings, where Tutankhamun's tomb was discovered. Visit the magnificent Temple of Hatshepsut, Egypt's only female pharaoh, and the towering Colossi of Memnon. Afternoon sailing to Edfu.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner included

Day 5: Edfu Temple & Kom Ombo

Morning visit to the Temple of Edfu, the best-preserved cult temple in Egypt dedicated to the falcon god Horus. Afternoon stop at Kom Ombo, a unique double temple dedicated to Sobek (crocodile god) and Haroeris. Sail to Aswan.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner included

Day 6: Abu Simbel & Aswan Highlights

Optional early morning flight to Abu Simbel (recommended) or visit the High Dam, Unfinished Obelisk, and beautiful Philae Temple dedicated to Isis. Afternoon felucca ride on the Nile around Elephantine Island.

Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner included

Day 7: Departure from Aswan

Disembark from your Nile cruise. Transfer to Aswan Airport for your flight back to Cairo, connecting to your international departure. Optional add-on: Red Sea extension in Hurghada or Sharm El Sheikh.

Breakfast included
